Aloo Tikki

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes (boiled)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • Spices
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Mash potatoes.
  2. Add spices and mix.
  3. Shape into patties.
  4. Shallow fry until golden.
  5. Serve hot.
