Pav Bhaji

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes (boiled)
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion, 2 tomatoes
  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Butter
  • Pav (bread)

Instructions

  1. Mash boiled vegetables.
  2. Sauté onion and tomato.
  3. Add spices and vegetables.
  4. Cook and mash well.
  5. Toast pav with butter.
  6. Serve hot.
