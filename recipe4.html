Chole Chaat

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chole)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt and red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Take boiled chickpeas in a bowl.
  2. Add chopped onion and tomato.
  3. Add chutneys and spices.
  4. Mix everything well.
  5. Garnish with coriander and lemon juice.
  6. Serve fresh.
