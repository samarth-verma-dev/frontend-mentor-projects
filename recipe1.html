Rajma Chawal

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma (kidney beans)
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Salt and oil

Instructions

  1. Soak rajma overnight and pressure cook.
  2. Sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add rajma and cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
